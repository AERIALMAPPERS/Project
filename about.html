MAHARASHTRA COASTLINE

Maharashtra is the second most populous and third largest state of India. It spread over 307713 Sq. Km and situated over west coast of India. Maharashtra occupies the western and central part of the country and as per statistical data available, Maharashtra has a long coastline stretching 720 kilometers along the Arabian Sea, which is divided, among 6
Districts as under: Out of this 720 Kms coastal length 400 Kms length is naturally protected, & About 320 Kms length is vulnerable to erosion.
